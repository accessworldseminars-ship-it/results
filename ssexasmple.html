The Session: Trace's Story

A 22-year-old from Virginia. DoorDashing to pay off a patent. Stuck. This is what happened.

The Starting Point

I was speaking with Trace, a twenty-two-year-old from Richmond, Virginia. He was in "crunch time" — working DoorDash to make the final payment on a patent. On the surface, he had a clear goal: find a stable, structured job.

But underneath, there was a familiar fog of procrastination and a lingering feeling of being "stuck," despite a recent retreat that had given him a taste of true euphoria.

When I asked him what he would create with a magic wand, he didn't ask for millions of dollars. He asked for stability in finances and in relationships. He wanted to feel that euphoria again — that state of being fully present. He had the resources. He knew the next step. But something was blocking him.

"My mind makes it out to be so much more than it is. I've been struggling with procrastination."

— Trace, before the session

This is where we must shift from conscious motivation to unconscious support.

The Process: Inner Child Healing & NSR

You cannot think your way out of a feeling that is lodged in your nervous system. You have to go in and clear it. This is what Nervous System Reprogramming (NSR) is all about.

I guided Trace through an inner child healing process — one of the most powerful ways to clear old patterns. I asked him to close his eyes and drift back to a time of pure joy, between the ages of four and eight. He found himself inside, with his brother and his mom, in the daytime.

We didn't just observe that little boy. We engaged with him. I guided Trace to take that joyful child by the hand and whisper into his ear — to tell that little boy everything he needed to know. That no matter what — through boredom, loneliness, or feeling unworthy — everything would be okay. That he would never be alone.

As Trace held that younger version of himself, the boy shrank and fit perfectly into his heart. Then we moved through time — looking at his life from an adult perspective, then from a higher, God's-eye perspective, showing that child that challenges are just character-building. That hardship creates strength.

Finally, we moved toward a bright light. I asked Trace to dive into that light, to feel his body expand and merge with it — realising that he is that light. Pure intelligence and truth.

The Result: Freedom from the Past

After the process, I asked Trace to try and feel bad about a past memory. Just try.

"I couldn't do it. It wasn't working. It was like good things just kept popping up."

— Trace, immediately after the process

He reported feeling "light."

This is the hallmark of emotional clearing. It's not about forgetting the past — it's about removing its charge. We don't get freedom from the past by thinking about it differently. We get freedom by reprocessing it at a neurological level, allowing the body to release the weight it's been holding.

When the past no longer dictates your present, you are finally free to choose your future.

Key Teaching Points

What this session reveals about the way real change actually works.

The Real Barrier

Procrastination isn't a character flaw — it's often a sign of unconscious resistance. The conscious mind wants the goal. The unconscious is holding onto old patterns or fears.

Reframing Rejection

Every "no" is a step closer to the "yes." Get excited when you hear no — you're ticking off attempts until you succeed. Internal fortitude makes the path shorter, not longer.

The Inner Child Connection

Many adult struggles — feeling unworthy, lonely, or stuck — have their roots in childhood. To change the adult, we must first comfort and empower the child within.

Unconscious Support

Conscious motivation is fleeting. Lasting change happens when you gain the support of your unconscious mind. You stop trying to do things — and simply find yourself doing them.

The "Light" Feeling

A sign of successful emotional clearing is physical lightness and an inability to feel negative emotions about past events. When it's gone, it's genuinely gone.

Freedom to Choose

When the past no longer dictates your present, you are finally free to choose your future. Not because you willed it — because the weight has been removed.
