The Two Types of Coach

Which one do you
want to become?

Most coaching training produces one type of coach. This training produces the other. The difference is everything.

Method 1 📚

The Information Coach

The coach has all the answers. They talk more than they listen. They give advice, share frameworks, and send clients away with things to read. The coach becomes the expert — and the client becomes dependent on the expert.

True transformation doesn't come from following a guru. It comes from within the client. When coaches make themselves the answer, they steal the power they're supposed to be giving away.

Method 2 — What You'll Master 🎯

The Implementation Coach

The coach listens deeply, finds out exactly what the client needs, and then takes them by the hand to get it. Every tool is deployed in service of the client's own inner wisdom — not the coach's.

The tools are already within your clients. Your job is to unlock them. This training teaches you exactly how — with frameworks, live demonstrations, and real-world practice built in from day one.
