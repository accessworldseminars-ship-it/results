⚡ How Concurrent Hours Work

One of the most powerful features of the AccessWorld certification model is that your training hours run concurrently across all five certifications. You are not required to repeat the same training five times.

1-on-1 Coaching — Every session counts toward all five certifications simultaneously.

Group Coaching & Mentoring — Each session you attend credits all programmes you are enrolled in at once.

Tool Development & Training — NLP modules, the World Class Coach session, and cross-programme training apply to all five certifications concurrently.

A participant pursuing multiple certifications simultaneously can complete shared training once and have those hours recognised across every applicable programme.

★ Best Practice — From Joshua Roy

I encourage every participant to attend as many live sessions as possible. There is a quality of learning, presence, and transformation in a live room that recordings cannot fully replicate. Show up. Be in the room. Engage.

Between live sessions, actively watch recordings, listen to podcasts, and engage with any training material that feeds your growth. Every hour you invest counts. Use replays and self-directed study to keep moving forward.

The goal is not hours. The goal is to develop yourself beyond the average person — to become exceptional, exemplary, and outstanding, both as an individual and as a coach.

The world does not need more average coaches. It needs coaches who have done the work — who show up with depth, skill, and the kind of earned authority that only comes from genuine personal investment.

All training components — seminars, webinars, group sessions, NLP modules — can be attended live or accessed via recording. Both count equally toward your certification hours. This means you can fit your learning around your life without falling behind.
Hours run concurrently across all five certifications. One coaching session, one group mentoring attendance, one training module — credits to every programme you are enrolled in simultaneously. You do not repeat the same training to earn multiple certificates.
Submit the specific NLP process only — closed-eye, set to music if desired. Do not submit a full coaching session. The recording demonstrates your ability to guide a client through an NLP intervention cleanly and effectively. Sessions can be done live or recorded.
A 3-month morning routine commitment of 1 hour, 5 days per week is required as a personal practice for all programmes. This does not count toward training hours — it counts toward becoming the kind of person who genuinely coaches from lived experience.
All programmes offer two coaching paths:
  • 1-on-1 Coaching: 12 sessions over 6 months (most programmes)
  • 1-on-1 Coaching: 24 sessions over 6+ months (Trauma only)
  • Group Mentoring: 24 sessions × 90 mins each (most programmes)
  • Group Mentoring: 48 sessions × 90 mins each (Trauma only)
Most programmes include external practice — visioning sessions for others, relationship demonstrations, healing process practice. These are required but do not count toward training hours. They count toward genuine competency. Practice on real people. It is the most important part.
Every certification begins with the Emotional Mastery 2-Day Seminar. This is the flagship programme of AccessWorld Seminars and the non-negotiable foundation for all five pathways. It is not optional. It is where everything begins.
