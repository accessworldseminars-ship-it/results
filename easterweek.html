The Holy Week

One week that
changed eternity.

The Holy Week — the final seven days of the Saviour's mortal life — is the most sacred period in all of human history. From His triumphant entry into Jerusalem on Palm Sunday, to His suffering in Gethsemane, His crucifixion on Golgotha, and His glorious resurrection on the third day, every moment carries profound meaning for each of us.

This Easter, our ward will walk through that sacred week together — station by station, story by story — in an evening designed to deepen our understanding, strengthen our faith, and draw us closer to the One whose Atonement makes all things possible.

Each stall will bring a different moment of the Holy Week to life — through interactive activities, messages, and experiences that will move hearts of all ages.
