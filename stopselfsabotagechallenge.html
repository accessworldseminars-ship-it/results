What Is Self Sabotage?

Getting In Your Own Way

Self sabotage is when you consciously want something — success, love, health, happiness — but unconsciously do things that block you from achieving it. It's the voice that says "I'll start tomorrow," the fear that stops you from speaking up, the procrastination that keeps you stuck.

The good news? Self sabotage is a learned pattern — and it can be unlearned.

Procrastination on important tasks
Negative self-talk and doubt
Fear of success or failure
Starting projects but never finishing
Repeating the same mistakes
Playing small when you know you're capable of more
