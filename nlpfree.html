What Is NLP?

Neuro-Linguistic Programming

NLP is the study of how we use language (both verbal and internal) and mental patterns to shape our experiences, habits, and results in life. Developed in the 1970s by Richard Bandler and John Grinder, NLP offers a series of models and techniques that allow you to update the unconscious programs that drive your behaviors — just like upgrading the software in your computer or phone.

In simple terms, NLP gives you the manual for your mind.
