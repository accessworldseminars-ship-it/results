What Is NSR?

Most people try to change their behaviour through willpower. It doesn't last. That's because the real driver of your patterns, reactions and results isn't your conscious mind — it's your nervous system.

NSR — Nervous System Reprogramming — is my proprietary methodology that goes beneath thought to reset the automatic programs driving your life. When your nervous system changes, everything changes: your emotions, your decisions, your performance, your relationships.
