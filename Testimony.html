Overcame Workplace Burnout & Boundary Issues

Burnout Boundaries Career
Before

I was in a cycle of exhaustion and emotional overwhelm, especially in my professional life. I was faced with an unsustainable 16-hour workday with no support, which led to a total meltdown—crying uncontrollably for an hour. I felt helpless, taken advantage of, and deeply alone.

What I Learned / Applied

I learned about the concept of Shadow Values—the hidden drivers behind my actions. We discovered that a core shadow value for me is Righteousness: my deep need to do the "right" thing and be someone who can be counted on, no matter the cost. I also uncovered that my actions often come with an unspoken expectation of Reciprocity and Validation.

Results

The biggest result was clarity. Naming these shadow values allowed me to understand my own patterns. Instead of just feeling helpless or resentful, I now see the underlying mechanics. I understand why I push myself to the brink and why I feel so let down when support isn't mutual. This awareness is the first and most powerful step.

"Understanding my Shadow Values turned my breakdown into a breakthrough. Now I know why I get overwhelmed and can choose a new path."

Healed Daughter Relationship & Financial Panic

Family Emotional Healing Financial Stress
Before

I was struggling deeply with my daughter's rejection, feeling a desperate urgency to fix the relationship immediately. The pain of not knowing why she didn't want to see me was overwhelming. I was also panicking about my financial situation, feeling like I was going backwards and that everything was crumbling at once.

What I Learned / Applied

I learned the principle of "the long game." My mentor shared that rebuilding authentic trust requires 18 months of consistent change away from the old environment—it can't be rushed. I applied the powerful tool of visualization: instead of dwelling in the present pain, I imagined a detailed, positive reunion with my daughter five years in the future, creating a "future memory" to hold onto.

Results

I found immediate relief from the pressure to solve everything now. Adopting the "long game" mindset gave me permission to be patient and focus on my own steady progress. The visualization technique replaced cycles of sadness with a tangible source of hope. Financially, I was able to shift from panic to a grounded assessment of my actual assets and stability.

"Learning about 'the long game' and using visualization gave me hope and a strategy. I'm no longer panicking to fix things now, and I understand the dynamics at play, which has taken a huge weight off me."

Overcame Debilitating Anxiety

Anxiety Freedom Technique Transformation
Before

My anxiety had become completely debilitating. It was preventing me from leaving the house and engaging in normal activities—I was even relying on online shopping for necessities. I felt stuck in a constant state of worry, catastrophizing about everything from my mother's potential dementia to everyday social interactions.

What I Learned / Applied

I learned that anxiety is fundamentally my brain catastrophizing about the future, and that it literally cannot coexist with present-moment awareness. My coach guided me through a powerful process called the Ultimate Freedom Technique. I identified the anxiety as a physical shape and color in my body, dialogued with it to understand its protective intent, and then consciously released it.

Results

The shift was profound. Immediately after the session, I felt lighter and had a tangible sense of hope about my future. The process helped me release a stored, heavy energy I'd been carrying. I moved from feeling paralyzed by "what ifs" to seeing a clear path forward where I could be involved in my community, enjoy deeper connections, and actually look forward to each day.

"The Ultimate Freedom Technique helped me release the heavy anxiety I was carrying. I went from feeling paralyzed to having a real blueprint for a future filled with freedom and connection."

Inner Child Empowerment Gray Room Process
Before

I learned the Gray Room process. Josh taught us to use play-pretend and our imagination to rewrite our stories. It totally worked. I felt empowered by the memories that I could get rid of. It was my decision to rewrite my stories—what to keep and what not to keep. I thought, 'My God, this is like magic. "The Gray Room process was like magic. It showed me I'm in control of my happiness and my thought patterns. It's become my superpower for continuous growth."
